About Angel

A strategist who has
lived the stakes.

Angel Carroll is a strategist, executive, and advocate who moves criminal-justice policy from the inside and the outside — pairing hard-won lived experience with more than a decade of coalition, campaign, and policy work.

Her story

Angel's path to policy didn't start in a statehouse. It started with the systems that shaped her own life — and a refusal to let anyone else navigate them alone.

She built her career at the intersection of proximity and power: organizing statewide campaigns, advising government agencies and civic leaders, and designing programs for the people most affected by the criminal-legal system. Along the way she founded MXD Company, a strategy practice built on a single conviction — that the people closest to a problem are closest to its solution.

Today she works across three registers at once: the courtroom, the campaign war room, and the culture. She is equally fluent translating policy for legislators, mobilizing a coalition, and meeting an audience where it actually is — on air, on stage, and online. That range is the point. Durable change requires all three.

Based between Houston and Austin, Angel partners with agencies, advocacy organizations, political leaders, and brands who want more than a headline — they want work that holds.
